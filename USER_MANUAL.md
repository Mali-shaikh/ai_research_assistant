# 🔬 AI Academic Research Assistant - User Manual

Welcome to your AI-powered research companion. This guide will help you navigate the interface and make the most of the academic analysis tools.

---

## 1. Getting Started: Authentication 🔐
Before you can upload or analyze documents, you need to create an account or log in.
- **Register**: Enter your name, email, and a secure password.
- **Login**: Use your credentials to access your private document library.
- **Status**: Look for the "Login Successful" message to confirm your session is active.

## 2. Document Management 📄
The **Upload & Documents** tab is your central hub for managing research papers.
- **Upload**: Support for `PDF`, `DOCX`, `TXT`, and `MD` files. Drag and drop your file and click **Upload Document**.
- **Library**: Use the dropdown menu to see all your uploaded papers.
- **Refresh**: If you don't see a recent upload, click the **🔄 Refresh** button.

## 3. Chatting with your Research 💬
Engage in a conversation with your documents using the **Chat** tab.
- **Select Document**: (Optional) Pick a specific paper from the dropdown to focus the AI's attention.
- **Interactive Chat**: Type questions like:
  - *"Summarize the main findings of this paper."*
  - *"What methodology was used?"*
  - *"Does this study mention clinical trials?"*
- **General AI**: If no document is selected, the assistant acts as a standard academic AI using Gemini Pro.

## 4. Specialized Analysis Tools ⚖️
### ⚖️ Compare Papers
Located in the **Compare Papers** tab. Select two different documents from your library and click **Compare**. The AI will generate a detailed side-by-side analysis of:
- Objectives & Hypotheses
- Methodology
- Results & Conclusions

### 📑 Extract Citations
When chatting, you can ask specifically for citations:
- *"List all citations from this paper."*
- *"Who are the primary authors referenced?"*

---

> **Pro Tip**: Use the **Refresh** buttons in each tab after uploading new documents to ensure the dropdown menus are up to date!
